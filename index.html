WHY WE Rock?

We are a Peterborough based cover band, famed for our ability to cover a wide variety of music. We pride ourselves on covering old and new pop/rock music and bring life to any party. Cosmic Rodney will travel within 100 miles of Peterborough to bring the music to you. So get in touch and let us help you dance the night away!
