WHY WE Rock?

Cosmic Rodney is a band based in Peterborough. They are famed for their ability to cover a wide variety of music. We pride ourselves on covering old and new pop/rock music and bring life to the party. We travel within 100 miles of our base to bring the music to you. So get in touch and let us help you dance the night away.
